About me

I’m a computer vision and deep learning researcher with strong expertise in AI and software engineering. I’m currently pursuing a PhD at Polytech Tours, France, where I develop automated fact-checking applications. Alongside my research, I’ve been a lecturer at the Faculty of Engineering, Technology, and Communication, Hong Duc University since October 2025, teaching and mentoring students in advanced computing. Previously, I worked as an AI and Software Engineer at ThinkLabs JSC, contributing to the design and deployment of AI-powered products.
