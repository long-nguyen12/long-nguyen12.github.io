About Me

I’m a computer vision and deep learning researcher with strong expertise in AI and software engineering. I’m currently pursuing a PhD at Polytech Tours, France, where I develop automated fact-checking applications. Alongside my research, I’ve been a lecturer at the Faculty of Engineering, Technology, and Communication, Hong Duc University since October 2025.

Research motivation

My research is driven by a passion for leveraging AI to solve real-world problems. I’m particularly interested in developing applications that can enhance information integrity and combat misinformation. Through my work, I aim to contribute to the advancement of AI technologies while addressing critical societal challenges.

Research interests

My research interests include computer vision, deep learning, automated fact-checking, and natural language processing. I’m particularly focused on developing innovative algorithms and applications that can improve the accuracy and efficiency of information verification processes.
